User Stories:
- Player and computer each get 7 cards, rest of deck is faced down in pile
- Player asks for a card they have in their possesion for matching
    - if computer does not have it, player picks up a card from the deck
        - if player gets requested card, they may request another
        - if player gets any other card, it is the computer's turn
    - if computer does have it, player gets it/them and gets another turn
